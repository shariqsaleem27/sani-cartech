FAQ

Answers to Your Questions

Everything you need to know about our mobile auto electrical services in Geelong.

What areas do you service?
We service all of Geelong and surrounds including Ocean Grove, Torquay, Armstrong Creek, Charlemont, Waurn Ponds, Belmont, Highton, Newtown and neighbouring suburbs. If you're unsure, give us a call and we'll let you know.
Do you come to my location?
Yes — we're a fully mobile auto electrician. We come to your home, workplace, or roadside. Our van is equipped with dealer-level diagnostics and the parts needed for most common repairs.
How much does a diagnostic cost?
Diagnostic fees vary depending on the complexity of the issue. We provide upfront pricing before starting any work, so there are no surprises. Call us to discuss your situation and we'll give you an honest quote.
Do you work on European vehicles?
Absolutely. European vehicles are one of our specialities. We have dealer-level diagnostic tools for Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Audi, Volkswagen, Land Rover and Range Rover. We handle everything from basic electrical faults to complex coding and programming.
Can you install dash cams and accessories?
Yes. We professionally install dash cams, reverse cameras, parking sensors, UHF radios, LED lighting and other aftermarket accessories. All wiring is done to a clean, factory-finish standard with no visible cables.
Do you offer same-day or emergency callouts?
We do our best to accommodate same-day and emergency callouts, especially for no-start and breakdown situations. Call us as early as possible and we'll work to fit you in. Emergency auto electrician services are available across Geelong.
