Our services

We offer a full range of auto electrical solutions designed to keep your vehicle running safely, reliably, and efficiently.

Mobile Auto Electrician

On-site diagnostics, breakdown repairs and no-start fault diagnosis. We come to your home, office or roadside across Geelong and surrounds.

Car Electrical Diagnostics

Advanced fault finding with CANBUS, ECU and BCM diagnostics. We trace wiring faults and solve intermittent electrical issues.

European Vehicle Specialist

Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Audi, VW, Land Rover and Range Rover. Expert diagnostics and coding for complex European electrical systems.

Battery & Charging Systems

Battery testing and replacement, alternator and starter motor diagnostics, and charging system fault repairs across Geelong.

Trailer Wiring & Towbar Electrics

7-pin and 12-pin trailer plug installation, trailer light wiring, electric brake controllers and complete towbar wiring solutions.

Dash Cam & Accessories

Dash cam, reverse camera, parking sensor installation. Plus UHF radios, LED lighting and aftermarket accessory wiring to a factory finish.

Car Air Conditioning

Air conditioning regas, leak detection, compressor diagnostics and climate control electrical repairs to keep you cool on the road.

ECU Coding & Programming

Module coding, key programming, ECU and BCM replacement and setup, plus software updates. Dealer-level capability at a fraction of the cost.

General Electrical Repairs

Headlight and taillight faults, indicator issues, interior electrical problems, fuse and relay diagnostics. No job too small.

Frequently asked questions

What areas do you service?
We service all of Geelong and surrounds including Ocean Grove, Torquay, Armstrong Creek, Charlemont, Waurn Ponds, Belmont, Highton, Newtown and neighbouring suburbs.
How quickly can you come out?
We offer same-day service for most jobs, especially breakdowns and no-start situations. Call us as early as possible and we'll work to fit you in the same day.
Do you work on all vehicle makes and models?
Yes. We work on all makes and models including European vehicles like Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Audi, VW, Land Rover and Range Rover. We carry dealer-level diagnostic tools for all major brands.
How much does a diagnostic cost?
Diagnostic fees vary depending on the complexity of the issue. We always provide upfront pricing before starting any work so there are no surprises. Call for a quote specific to your situation.
Can you supply and fit parts?
Yes, we can source and supply quality parts for most common repairs. Our mobile workshop carries a range of batteries, relays, fuses, wiring components and other parts to complete jobs on the spot.
