Service overview

Your vehicle's electrical system is the backbone of everything — from starting the engine to powering your lights, windows and safety systems. When something goes wrong, you need an auto electrician who can come to you, diagnose the issue fast, and get it fixed right. Our fully equipped mobile workshop carries dealer-level scan tools, parts and wiring supplies so most jobs are completed in a single visit — no towing, no workshop drop-offs, no waiting around.

Frequently asked questions

What areas does your mobile service cover?
We service all of Geelong and surrounds including Ocean Grove, Torquay, Armstrong Creek, Charlemont, Waurn Ponds, Belmont, Highton, Newtown and neighbouring suburbs. If you're unsure, give us a call.
How quickly can you come out for a breakdown?
We offer same-day callouts for breakdowns and no-start situations. Call us as early as possible and we'll work to fit you in — often within a couple of hours depending on our schedule and your location.
Do I need to tow my car to a workshop?
No — that's the whole point of our mobile service. We come to you with a fully equipped van, whether you're at home, work, or on the roadside. Most repairs are completed on-site in a single visit.
How much does a mobile callout cost?
Pricing varies depending on the job. We always provide an upfront quote before starting any work so there are no surprises. Call us to discuss your situation and we'll give you an honest estimate.
What vehicles do you work on?
We work on all makes and models — from everyday cars to European vehicles, 4WDs, utes and light commercial vehicles. We carry dealer-level diagnostic tools for all major brands.
